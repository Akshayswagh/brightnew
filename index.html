About Us

At Bright Cabs, we believe in sustainable and smart mobility. Our fleet includes a mix of sedans, MUVs, and electric vehicles to support green transportation initiatives.

Bright Urban Mobility Private Limited, popularly known as Bright Cabs, is a trusted name in corporate commute and employee transportation services since 1997. Formerly known as Om Sai Travels, established in 1997 and reconstituted as Bright Urban Mobility Private Limited in the year of 2022.

With over two decades of experience, we have been at the forefront of transforming how organizations move their people safely, punctually, and efficiently..

Headquartered in Kharadi, Pune, Maharashtra, Bright Cabs specializes in providing customized transport solutions to leading corporates, IT/ITES companies, and business parks across India. Our commitment to safety, reliability, and round-the-clock service has earned us long-standing partnerships with some of the most respected names in the industry.

From deploying GPS-enabled fleets to offering real-time ride tracking and automated rostering, we combine technology with dependable service to ensure seamless mobility for employees and peace of mind for our clients.
